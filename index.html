About Aether Robotics

Aether Robotics operates at the intersection of innovation and necessity, with primary operations in the United States and expanding presence in Japan.

We specialize in humanoid robotics and nanorobotics, developing solutions that address critical challenges in healthcare, environmental sustainability, and industrial automation.

Our approach combines cutting-edge technology with thoughtful design, creating products that enhance human capability rather than replace it.
